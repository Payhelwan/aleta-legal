**Privacy Policy for Aleta** **Effective Date:** [02-05-2026] Welcome to Aleta! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Aleta mobile application. We have written this policy in clear, plain English to ensure you fully understand how your data is handled. Aleta is the data controller for the personal data collected through the app. By using Aleta, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. --- ### 1. Information We Collect To provide you with a personalized habit-tracking experience and power your AI companion, we collect the following types of information: **A. Information You Provide to Us** * **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. We use Google’s Firebase Authentication to manage this securely. * **Habit and App Data:** We collect the data you input to use the app, such as your habits, daily progress, and interactions with your AI creature companion. **B. Information Collected Automatically** * **Usage Data:** We use Google Analytics to collect basic information about how you interact with Aleta. This includes your device type, operating system, app version, screen views, and session length. This data is anonymized and helps us understand how the app is being used so we can improve it. **C. Information We DO NOT Collect** We believe in minimizing data collection. Aleta **does not** collect, request, or access: * Precise geographical location (GPS data). * Your phone’s contact list or address book. * Medical or sensitive health information. --- ### 2. How We Use Your Information Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we must have a legal basis to process your personal data. We use your information for the following purposes based on the corresponding legal grounds: * **To Provide and Maintain the Service (Performance of a Contract):** We use your account info and habit data to let you log in, save your progress, and allow your AI companion to grow and reward you. * **To Improve the App (Legitimate Interests):** We analyze usage data via Google Analytics to identify bugs, understand user trends, and improve the overall app experience and user interface. * **To Communicate with You (Consent/Legitimate Interests):** We may use your email address to send you important updates regarding your account, security notices, or changes to this policy. --- ### 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services **We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.** We only share your data with trusted third-party service providers that help us operate the app. These providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and are not allowed to use your data for their own purposes: * **Firebase (by Google):** Used for secure user authentication and database storage.
